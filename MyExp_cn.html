I am Yutong Zhao, a physics master student currently study in Univeristy of Manitoba in Canada. You can find my resume here.

A little history: My hometown Langfang is a satellite city of Beijing which is located approximately midway between Beijing and Tianjin and its population is 4.74 million. The name of Langfang could be traced back to Song dynasty with more than one thousand years history (995 - 1000 A.D., named after the Lv duan, a famous Prime Minister of Song dynasty when he is the Undersecretary of finance, meaning the "The House of Undersecretary"), the Guangyang district where my home is located has a even longer history which can be traced back to the year of 222 B.C. The Qin dynasty first set the Guangyang county there.

A little geology: It is also known as the "Beijing-Tianjin Corridor" which has a 20 min long by high speed train to both Beijing and Tianjin. The newly built Beijing Daxing International Airport is partially located in Langfang. My mother is a assosiate professor of North China Institute of Aerospace Engineering and My father is a senior instructor of Langfang Technician School.
