选择物理学的原因

My original idea to study physics is very simple. As a little kid with little knowledge and full of curiosity, trying to understand the universe is his first priority at his spear time.

In the darkest time of his early life, nature science including but not limited to Physics helped the little kid overcome the fear of unknown. Nowadays, the kid is no more young but still carries lots of curiosity.
